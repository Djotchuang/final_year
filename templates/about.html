About Us

What is NaFake?

NaFake is a Fake News Detection platform. It's model is built with the Passive Aggressive Classifier Machine Learning algorithm.
You can use the detector in any of these ways:

  • Fill in the title and news text in the fields provided and click/press the verify button.
  • Insert the url of the news in the box provided and click/press verify.

Motivation

The Faculty of Engineering and Technology of the University of Buea assigned this project to me as my Final Year project in Partial Fulfillment of a B. Eng Degree in Computer (Software) Engineering.

In 2016, during the United States election, a lot of fake news websites were created, and shared through social media, especially on Facebook. They were so much so that, Fake News had more clicks than the real ones.

One of the most iconic cases were from a citzen from Macedonia who had over 100 fake news websites, and earned thousands of dollars with advertising.

Most of those websites were pro-Trump, why? Was the macedonian a true Trump defender? Not necessarily! But he noticed that Trump voters were more likely to believe and propagate Fake News.

The Fake News Detector is an initiative to make some difference in the fight against this problem - joining your effort (Crowdsourcing) with technology (Machine Learning).
